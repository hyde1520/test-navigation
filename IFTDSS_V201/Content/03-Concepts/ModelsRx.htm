Create Burn Plan Document

The burn plan document pathway was designed using the recommendations of the Interagency Prescribed Fire Planning and Implementation Procedures Guide (U. S. Department of Agriculture and U. S. Department of the Interior, 2014). The pathway consists of 21 elements (such as objectives and ignition plan).

You can fill in each IFTDSS page in this pathway; once it is complete, you can download the plan as a Microsoft Word document. Once the burn plan is created and downloaded, you can easily update and/or customize all sections of the burn plan using Microsoft Word.

Please watch the video to walk through the steps of Creating a Burn Plan Document.
