Response Functions (Definitions)

Response functions describe the effect of fire on the values-at-risk.

Response functions are mathematical relationships between fire characteristic (e.g., flame lengthThe flame length of a spreading surface fire within the flaming front is measured from midway in the active flaming combustion zone to the average tip of the flames.
