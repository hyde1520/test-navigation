Briefing

All assigned personnel must be briefed at the beginning of each operational period to ensure personnel safety.

Prescribed fire objectives and operations must be clearly defined and understood.

The briefing checklist should list briefing topics only, not re-state what is listed in the Prescribed Fire Plan for that element.

The Prescribed Fire Burn Boss is to ensure that any new personnel arriving at the prescribed fire site receives a briefing prior to assignment.
