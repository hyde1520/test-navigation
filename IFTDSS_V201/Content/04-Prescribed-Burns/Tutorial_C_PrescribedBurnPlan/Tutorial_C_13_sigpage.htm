Signature Page (Element 1)

Navigate to Element 1: Signature Page.

The following information must be included on the signature page:

  1. Administrative unitA unit encompasses a single or group of fuelbeds that are part of a fuel treatment (e.g., harvest unit, prescribed burn unit) or wildland fire. name
  2. Prescribed fire name
  3. At a minimum, three dated signatures are required:
    • Prescribed burn preparer
    • Technical Reviewer
    • Agency Administrator
  4. Final determined complexity rating

(USDA and U.S. DOI, 2014, p. 24)
