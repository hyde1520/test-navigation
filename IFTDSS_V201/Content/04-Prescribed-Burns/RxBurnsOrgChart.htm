Burn Plan Element 11: An Organization Chart

On this page is an example organization chart, which is provided in a Word 2010 template. (You can use a locally developed organization chart in place of this chart.)

Using the Word 2010 template, add boxes as needed for additional personnel assigned for each operational period.

The organization chart may be completed on the day of the burn and must be completed for each operational period.
