Tree Mortality Input Parameters

Name Units Brief Description
Crown Ratio A ratio The ratio of live crown length to total tree height. In this system, whole number codes are used (for example, 4 indicates that the live crown length is 40% of tree height).
Diameter Breast HeightTree diameter at breast height. Because trees are generally wider at the base and narrower higher up, the diameter at breast height is used to define the size of the tree. The definition of breast height can vary but is usually 4.5 feet above the ground. Inches, centimeters Diameter at breast height expressed in inches or centimeters. Breast height is 4.5 ft or 1.37 meters above the ground.
Fire Severity drop-down choice Fire severity: 1 – low, 2 – moderate, 3 – high, 4 – very high, 5 – extreme.
Flame Length Feet, meters Flame length is the distance from the ground at the leading edge of the flame to the tip of the flame.
Scorch Height Feet, meters The average height of foliage browning or boleThe trunk of a tree. (tree trunk) blackening caused by a fire.
Tree Height Feet, meters The above-ground height of individual trees, or groups of trees, from the tree base to tree top. Tree height can be expressed in feet or meters.
Tree Species NA The species name of the individual tree(s) in question.
Trees per Acre Trees per acreA measure of land used in the Imperial and US systems; 43,560 square feet or 4,840 square yards. There are 640 acres in one square mile. One acre is 0.405 hectares.
