Note

Fire behavior values are calculated on a pixel-by-pixel basis and are simulated independently with respect to the surrounding pixels in the landscape input file. The burning potential of the pixel, or point location, being assessed is not affected by fire behavior in adjacent locations, roads, or hazardous fuel types in proximity to the area under assessment.
