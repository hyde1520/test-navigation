Canopy Height Feet, meters Canopy height or stand height for fire behavior modeling is the average height of the dominant or co-dominant tree layer in a stand. Canopy height is not the height of the tallest tree in the stand or an average height of all trees within the given area. Canopy height is used in fire behavior modeling for computing the reduction of 20-ft wind speeds to midflame wind speeds and for computing spotting distances from torching trees.
