Cost of Resources Used Dollars The total cost of all resources dispatched to the fire. If a resource is dispatched to a fire, the resource cost is the resource base cost plus the resource hourly cost for the time spent on the fire until the fire is contained or the resource duration expires.
Fire Area at Initial Attack Acres, hectares, meters squared The fire size when the first resource begins suppression action on the fire. Calculated from the fire size at report and the resource arrival time.
Fireline Constructed Chains, feet, meters The total length of firelineA fireline (also called a fireguard or firebreak) is a trip of open land (cleared or plowed to the mineral soil) that is used to stop the spread of a fire. constructed by all resources at the time the fire was contained or determined to have escaped. If the fire is not successfully contained, fireline constructed is the total length of fireline constructed by all resources at the time the last available resource duration expires. If the fire is successfully contained, fireline constructed is the total length of line constructed by all resources at the time of containment.
Number of Resources Used Whole numbers Indicates those resources actually used to contain the fire. Number of resources used may be less than the number of resources specified on the worksheet if the fire is contained before all resources arrive.
Perimeter at Initial Attack Chains, feet, meters Perimeter at initial attack is the perimeter of the elliptically shaped fire when the first resource begins suppression action on the fire. Calculated from the fire size at report and the resource arrival time.
Time from Report Hour The elapsed time from when the fire was reported until the fire was either successfully contained or escaped the initial attack made by the dispatched resources.
