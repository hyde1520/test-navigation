From a Firebrand

The required input variables for ignition from a firebrand are fuel temperature and firebrand ignition. The model provides the probability of ignition from a firebrand as a percentage (Schroeder, 1969).

From Lightning

The required input variables for ignition from lightning are forest floor characteristics and fuel moistureMoisture content in fuels determines whether and to what extent fuels will burn. Fuel moisture is a percentage calculated by the weight of contained water in the fuel divided by the ovendry weight of the fuel. data. The model provides the probability of ignition from a lightning strike as a percentage (Latham and Schlieter, 1989).
