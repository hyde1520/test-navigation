Relative Net Value Change (Default) NA Relative net change in land value due to fire is assumed to be a function of fire behavior, and includes both positive and negative effects of fire.

Net Value Change (NVC) = Overall Burn Probability * NVC Multiplier

By default, the NVC output value range is binned by 20% for each of five categories.

  • Greatest Loss/Least Benefit
  • High Loss/Low Benefit
  • Average Loss/Benefit
  • Low Loss/High Benefit
  • Least Loss/Greatest Benefit

For more information, see Relative Net Value Change.
