What can IFTDSS do for you?

IFTDSS is...

The Interagency Fuels Treatment Decision Support System (IFTDSSInteragency Fuels Treatment Decision Support System. A web-based software integration framework that organizes and extends previously existing fire and fuelsCombustable materials that a fire consumes in order to burn. The models in IFTDSS take fuelCombustable materials that a fire consumes in order to burn. The models in IFTDSS take fuel inputs such as trees, bushes, and grasslands. inputs such as trees, bushes, and grasslands. software applications to make fuels treatment planning and analysis more efficient and effective.) is a web-based modeling framework. IFTDSS users can acquire, edit, or upload fuels data (Table 1), and run a variety of models to determine fire behavior, effects, vegetation changes, or run risk and hazard analysis (Table 2). IFTDSS has been developed for fuels treatment specialists by fuels treatment specialists to make fuels treatment planning and analysis more efficient and effective.

Choose and run your models

Models in IFTDSS are chosen by Workflow. Workflows are categorized by the results or process you are looking to model. Workflow categories in IFTDSS include:

  • Hazard Analysis
  • Risk Assessment
  • Fuels Treatment
  • Prescribed burn planning
