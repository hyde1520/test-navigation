Getting Started (Instructions, Tutorials, and Videos)

Welcome to IFTDSSInteragency Fuels Treatment Decision Support System. A web-based software integration framework that organizes and extends previously existing fire and fuelsCombustable materials that a fire consumes in order to burn. IFTDSS provides data and tools to support fuels treatment analysis and planning. IFTDSS also includes tools to use when developing burn plans and burn plan documentation, and when performing a risk assessment.
