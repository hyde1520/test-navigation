Wind Speed

IFTDSS supports the entry of mid-flame wind speed, but does not give the option of entering 20-ft or 10-m wind speeds with a user-supplied wind speed adjustment factor (WAFWind adjustment factor. An adjustment to wind speed used when calculating fire spread and intensity.) or calculated WAF. IFTDSS always uses the maximum reliable effective wind speed (i.e., this approach cannot be disabled).
