Exporting data as raster layers to ArcMap for analysis

To download raster versions of the consume inputs and outputs, scroll down to the Data Sets box of the Run Summary page

Download the raster datasets of the consume inputs and outputs:

  1. Select Download under Actions
  2. Select your desired format (raster for this example). You will see the export progress indicated as a percent
  3. Once the export process has completed, the percent progress will be replaced by a Download icon. Click the download icon to download a zipfile.
