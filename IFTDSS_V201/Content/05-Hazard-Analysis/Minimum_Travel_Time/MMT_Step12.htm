Editing the Landscape

At this point we will edit the landscape to reflect the post treatment features. You may also choose to create treatment polygons at this point, but we have already created our Rx treatment polygon earlier in this tutorial.

For our example we will activate our Squaw Rx polygon by placing a check in the box next to Treatments under Barriers in the left hand layers menu and select the Polygon-based Advanced Edit tool at the top of the page and click on our polygon. This will open the Edit Feature dialog panel shown below. Here we will change our landscape features.
