Ignitions

The Next button will now take you to the Ignitions window. This is where you will draw your ignitions. You will notice in the mapping toolbar that the buttons that previously allowed us to create and modify polygons have been replaced with buttons to draw and modify ignitions.

Drawing an ignition is done in the same way that polygons are drawn, except in this case we will draw a line. Select the Draw polygon tool from the Mapping toolbar, left click on the point on the map where you want to begin the ignition, and double left click to end the ignition line.

We elected to draw only one small ignition point, but you may draw multiple ignitions. We placed our ignition on private land adjacent to our treatment area. Again if you name your ignition in the Save Ignition As box you will save it for future runs. Click Next when finished.
