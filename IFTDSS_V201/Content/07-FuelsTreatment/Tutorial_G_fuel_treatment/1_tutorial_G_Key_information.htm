  • Fire behavior values are calculated pixel-by-pixel and are simulated independently with respect to the surrounding pixels in the landscape input file.
  • Fire behavior in locations, roads, or hazardous fuel types in proximity to the area under assessment do not affect the burning potential of the pixel, or point location, being assessed. In other words, fire behavior in pixels surrounding fuels treatment units will not be affected by the fuels treatments.
