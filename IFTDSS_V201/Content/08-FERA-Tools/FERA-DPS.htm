Within IFTDSS, tools, workflows, and other elements., such as the Digital Photo Series (DPS). The Digital Photo Series (DPS) provides the Natural Fuels Photo Series data electronically. The data in the DPS include 42 photo series containing a total of 438 sites in a searchable database. The photo series is provided via a link to http://depts.washington.edu/nwfire/dps/. Help information for the DPS is also available through the same link.

You can link to the DPS from within IFTDSS to obtain fuel loadingDry weight in tons/acreA measure of land used in the Imperial and US systems; 43,560 square feet or 4,840 square yards. In that way, you can use the DPS to customize fuel loading information for your specific situation rather than use IFTDSS default values.

DPS fuel loading output parameters (which are manually transferred to IFTDSS) are

  • biomass loading
  • crown
  • crown area
  • density
  • depth
  • diameter at breast height
  • height
