Continuing research should provide improved quantification of the factors that control crown fire potential so that estimates of crown fire potential can be improved in future FCCS releases.

The weighted average of crown fire subpotentials are scaled to an index from 0 to 9. Crown fire potential (CFP) is calculated as follows:

CFP = (IC+TC/3+RC)/2.333
