TEMPERATURE AND RELATIVE HUMIDITY DATA
Air Temperature - Maximum Fahrenheit, Celsius The maximum air temperature throughout the duration of the fire.  Air temperature is the ambient dry bulb temperature measured in the shade.
Air Temperature - Minimum Fahrenheit, Celsius The minimum air temperature throughout the duration of the fire.  Air temperature is the ambient dry bulb temperature measured in the shade.
Relative Humidity - Maximum Percent The maximum relative humidity throughout the duration of the fire.  Relative humidity is the ratio of the amount of moisture in the air compared to the amount the air could hold if fully saturated (100%).
Relative Humidity - Minimum Percent The minimum relative humidity throughout the duration of the fire.  Relative humidity is the ratio of the amount of moisture in the air compared to the amount the air could hold if fully saturated (100%).
