The Planning Cycle - Reporting

The Reporting stage of the Planning Cycle will allow you to:

  • Produce Fuels Treatments
    • Based on Pre and Post Monitoring Data and Project Objectives
    • Based on FTEM Data
    • Based on On-Site Prescribed Burn Observations
  • Manage Business Needs
    • FACTS
    • NFPORS
  • Produce Success Stories
    • Using Story Maps
    • Using Templates

I want more detail on the Reporting stage

The IFTDSS development team is working behind the scenes to build out the Reporting capabilities. These functions will become available in a future IFTDSS release.
