Beginning a Project

If you have not done so already, create an IFTDSS account.

Creating an IFTDSS Account

Go to https://iftdss.firenet.gov/landing_page/Choose the Login option in the top right of the IFTDSS page and click Register at the bottom of the Login box.When the New User Registration page displays, enter the required information and click Register .Your User Name is displayed on many exportable IFTDSS reports, so pick one you don't mind sharing!Once your information is submitted, you receive an email from an IFTDSS administrator confirming your account has been created. You may log into IFTDSS at anytime after receiving this confirmation email.When logging in for the first time, you may be prompted to accept the IFTDSS IT Security Rules of Behavior.

There is no formal definition of 'project' for IFTDSS, rather, the term refers to the collection of landscape files, model runs, and associated data for your specific area. It is easier to keep track of your files in IFTDSS if you create a folder for your project first.

A. From My Workspace, accessed in the top navigation menu of IFTDSS:

Create a folder by clicking the Create Folder option at the top of the My Workspace page.

B. Give your folder a name, in this example 'Haypress Creek Project', and click Submit.

Your new folder will now be visible in the Folders list.
