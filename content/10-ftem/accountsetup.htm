FTEM Account Setup

These instructions assume you already have an IFTDSS account. Once you are logged into your IFTDSS account, you may request an FTEM account. Having this FTEM account will allow you to access the FTEM database.

If you do not have an IFTDSS account yet, create one
  1. Go to https://iftdss.firenet.gov/landing_page/

  2. Choose the Login option in the top right of the IFTDSS page and click Register at the bottom of the Login box.

  3. When the New User Registration page displays, enter the required information. Your User Name is displayed on many exportable IFTDSS reports, so pick one you don't mind sharing! When finished, click Register.

    When entering information into the "Justification" field, simply enter a sentence or less describing what you'd like to use IFTDSS for.

  4. After clicking Register you will see a confirmation message verifying your registration request has been submitted.

  5. Once your information is submitted, you receive an email from an IFTDSS administrator that contains an access code that you will use for your first time logging in. This code remains valid for 72 hours. You may now log into IFTDSS and set your password.

  6. When logging in for the first time, you may be prompted to accept the IFTDSS IT Security Rules of Behavior.

  7. After accepting the User Agreement you will prompted to enter your temporary access code one last time, and set up your password. When finished, click Set Password.

Request FTEM access for the first time

FTEM can be accessed from the Monitoring Stage of the Planning Cycle, or the top navigation

After selecting FTEM for the first time from the top navigation of IFTDSS, or from the Planning Cycle you are taken to the Request Access page.

  1. Select the agency your work for from the drop-down menu.
  2. Select your geographic area of interest from the drop-down menu.
  3. Provide a brief justification to help administrators approve your account.
  4. When finished, click the Request FTEM Access button.

You will receive a notice confirming your access request has been received. Once it has been approved, you will be notified via email and may then access the FTEM portion of IFTDSS.

This will allow you to access FTEM, but before you are able to use it, you must select your user role and get it approved.

Get your user role approved to start using FTEM

To establish, or change, your user role in FTEM, follow the steps below. You need to do this to ensure you have editing permissions in FTEM:

  1. From the FTEM page, click your username in the top right of the screen and select Profile.
  2. Select the My Roles tab.
  3. From the My Roles tab, click the Add FTEM Roles button.
  4. In the box that pops up, select your desired role for your corresponding agency by clicking the check box next the role.
  5. Provide a brief justification for this role.
  6. When finished, click the Request FTEM Roles button.

You will receive a notice confirming your User Role request has been received. Once it has been approved, you are notified via email.

Change your geographic area of interest in My Preferences
  1. From the FTEM page, click your username in the top right of the screen and select Profile
  2. Select the My Preferences tab.

  3. Click the Select New Map Extent button.

  4. Use the drop-down menu to select the desired agency and geographic area

  5. Click Submit
