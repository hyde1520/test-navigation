Create/Edit Shapes

About the Edit/Create Shapes Interface

Edit/Create Shapes allows you to create, edit, and save polygons (shape) in IFTDSS, that can be used as areas of interest or landscape masks.

Draw a Polygon in Map Studio with the extent, polygon, or freehand button and save.

Drag a Rectangle: To drag a rectangular extent, click the Extent button, navigate to the portion of the map where you intend to place the corner of the rectangle. Hold down the left mouse button, drag the cursor until you have your desired area highlighted in yellow, and release the left mouse button. To discard your polygon, use the Clear button . To edit your polygon, double left click anywhere within the area of the polygon. To save, give the polygon a name, specify the folder in which you would like to store the polygon, and click Create.

Snap Points: To snap a polygon using points, click the Polygon button, navigate to the portion of the map where you intend to place your polygon, and single left click the mouse to place your first point. Go the location where you want your second point, single left click again, and continue this process until you have drawn you polygon. Double left click to finish. To discard your polygon, use the Clear button. To edit your polygon, double left click anywhere within the area of the polygon. To save, give the polygon a name, specify the folder in which you would like to store the polygon, and click Create.

Draw Freehand: To draw a polygon freehand, click the Freehand button, navigate to the portion of the map where you intend to place your polygon, and hold down the left mouse key. While continuing to hold down the left mouse key, use the mouse to draw your polygon, and when you are finished, release the left mouse button. To discard your polygon, use the Clear button. To edit your polygon, double left click anywhere within the area of the polygon. To save, give the polygon a name, specify the folder in which you would like to store the polygon, and click Create.

Copy and Edit A Polygon in Map Studio

To Copy and Edit a shape:

  1. Open Edit Tools from the bottom of Map Studio and click Copy from Existing Features . To edit only one polygon, choose Select Singlepart, or to edit multiple polygons choose Select multipart.

  2. Scroll to the map and double left click the mouse button over the polygon you intend to edit. This displays the polygon vertices and an editing box.

  3. Move a vertex by hovering over it, holding down the left mouse key, and dragging it to the correct position.

  4. Delete a vertex by hovering over it, clicking the right mouse button once, and clicking Delete.

  5. Continue your edits until you are satisfied with your shape.

  6. When you are finished, save the corrections as a new shape by giving it a descriptive name in Edit Tools, specify the folder to save the file to, and click Create. The new shape will now be available in My Workspace for use throughout IFTDSS.

Trying to find a polygon on a landscape where you have many polygons displayed at once? Try filtering using the Attribute Table.

Any polygons saved with the Edit Tool will be permanently saved to the IFTDSS system and will be available in MyWorkspace, and the generate, edit, and summarize landscape interfaces.
