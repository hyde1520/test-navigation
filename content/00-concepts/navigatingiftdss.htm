IFTDSS Overview

There are four main components of the IFTDSS application. These can be accessed anytime, depending upon what you are looking to do, and all of them are available when you first log in. They include:

My Workspace

My Workspace: This is a list of all your files used in IFTDSS. These can be organized into folders. Clicking on the name of any file brings up options such as edit, delete, and view on map. When you first log in, My Workspace is available from 'Continue Working' . The files available in My Workspace include:

  • Landscape files (.lcp files) which is the basic file used throughout IFTDSS to represent your landscape.
  • Shapefiles that you upload and those that you create within IFTDSS
Modeling Playground

Modeling Playground: Here, you choose which models you'd like to run, and then begin modeling. All model outputs are also listed in My Modeling Playground. Clicking on a model output name brings up options such as edit, delete, view, Request a report (see results as tables and charts), or map (see results displayed on a map). You can get to this part of IFTDSS by clicking the Model Fire Behavior/Effects button from the log in page.

Map Studio

Map Studio: This is the mapping interface of IFTDSS, where you can create and edit landscape and shape files, view modeling outputs spatially, and compare various landscape features and modeling outputs across your landscape. This is available on navigation bar at the top of IFTDSS.

The Fuels Planning Cycle

The Fuels Planning Cycle: The planning cycle walks you through the steps of landscape planning, such as landscape evaluation, strategic planning, implementation, and reporting. Each stage of the planning cycle walks you through the steps necessary to complete it. Each step takes you to the part of the IFTDSS application needed to complete it. The Planning Cycle is available from the Start Here button when you first log into IFTDSS.

Help Status, and User Profiles

Though unrelated to landscape analysis, it is useful to know where you can get help in IFTDSS, check a run status, or make changes to your user profile:

Help

You may access the IFTDSS Help Center anytime by clicking the Help icon in the top right of the menu bar.Help Center - will take you to the Help Center main page and search bar. From here you can use the links, or search bar, to find full-length help pages, videos, tutorials, and technical documentation.About this Page - will take you to the specific topic in Help Center that addresses the page you're using in IFTDSS, generally a step by step walkthough with video links.As you use Help Center will notice Notes, Tips, and Important informationtext marked 'important' indicates information you need to know for the run or operation to successfully complete IFTDSS. a note includes helpful information that applies to the task you are performing.a tip includes information that will save time, or make subsequent processes in IFTDSS easiertext here describes features that will be added to IFTDSS in the near futureQ & A Forums - takes you to the IFTDSS Users forum where you can get help from other IFTDSS users. Help Tour - Displays short tips and links within the interface.There are also help embedded in the IFTDSS interface, either small question mark icons that display very brief tips and links when clicked, or help information displayed in the right-hand panel.

Status

Status Notifications let you know when modeling runs or other IFTDSS operations are successful, and when they fail.

User Profile

User Profile has prompts to:

  • Change your profile information such as email address
  • Change your password
  • Log Off of IFTDSS
