Setting Up an IFTDSS Account

To create a new account, start from the IFTDSS Login prompt: 

  1. Go to https://iftdss.firenet.gov/landing_page/.

  2. Choose Login at the top right of the IFTDSS page and click Register at the bottom of the Login box.

  3. When the New User Registration page displays, enter the required information. Your User Name is displayed on many exportable IFTDSS reports, so pick one you don't mind sharing! When finished, click Register.

  4. After clicking Register you will see a confirmation message verifying your registration request has been submitted.

  5. Once your information is submitted, you receive an email from IFTDSS that contains a temporary password valid for 72 hours. Cut and paste the temporary password into the password field when logging into IFTDSS for the first time.

  6. You will be prompted to accept the IFTDSS IT Security Rules of Behavior.

  7. After accepting the Rules of Behavior you will prompted to enter your temporary password one last time, and set up your new password. When finished, click Reset Password.

  8. You will then be returned to the IFTDSS login page, where you will log back in with your username and newly changed password.
