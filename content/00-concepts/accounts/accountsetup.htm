Setting Up an IFTDSSAccount

To create a new account, choose the Login option in the top right of the IFTDSS page and click Register at the bottom of the Login box.

When the New User Registration page displays, enter the required information and click Register .

Your User Name is displayed on many exportable IFTDSS reports, so pick one you don't mind sharing!

Once your information is submitted, you receive an email from an IFTDSS administrator confirming your account has been created. You may log into IFTDSS at anytime after receiving this confirmation email.

When logging in for the first time, you may be prompted to accept the IFTDSS IT Security Rules of Behavior.
