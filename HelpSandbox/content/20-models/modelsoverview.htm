Modeling in Modeling Playground

Modeling Playground is the area of IFTDSS to populate and run models. Model outputs are also available here, you may view summaries, or spatial outputs in Map Studio. You will find in IFTDSS that Modeling Playground, and the models therein, can be accessed from multiple parts of IFTDSS, such as accessing Landscape Fire Behavior from the Landscape Evaluation stage of the Planning Cycle for example.

As of this Spring 2017 Release of IFTDSS, modeling functions focus on landscape fire behavior. Available and future models will include:

  • Landscape Fire Behavior

    • The current capability of Modeling Playground is focused on fire behavior. It allows you to model various weather conditions and fuel moistures to ensure landscape fuel model parameters accurately generate expected fire behavior model outputs based on your knowledge and experience. This is also a place to experiment with “what if” scenarios.

  • Behave
  • Burn Probability for Quantitative Risk Assessment
  • And more....

The IFTDSS development team is working behind the scenes to build out the modeling capabilities. These functions will become available in a future IFTDSS releases.
