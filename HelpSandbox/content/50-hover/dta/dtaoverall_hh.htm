Develop alternatives to determine how different fuels treatments and their spatial arrangement affect landscape characteristics and fire behavior, generate maps and reports, compare to determine the best treatment options

  1. Select an unedited landscape (Originating Landscape) to define your area, and optionally an area of interest
  2. Bring in previously edited versions, or edit further to create an "Existing Landscape" and treatment alternative landscapes.
  3. Enter fire behavior and model inputs
  4. Run fire behavior models on landscapes
  5. Compare landscape characteristics and resultant fire behavior outputs between landscapes on maps and in reports.

All files generated will be stored in the same folder as your originating landscape in My Workspace.
