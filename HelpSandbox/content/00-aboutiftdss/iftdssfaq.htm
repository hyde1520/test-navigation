IFTDSS FAQS

What Can IFTDSS Do?
  • Download Landfire LCP files to your account:
    • Landfire 2012 Version
    • Landfire 2014 Version
  • Create or upload shapefiles to use as landscape masks/areas of interest
  • Edit Landfire layers to reflect your landscape or simulate treatments
  • Create Landscape Summary Reports of landscape features and automatically acquired 97th Percentile weather conditions
  • Model Landscape Fire Behavior
  • Simulate and analyze landscape treatment alternatives and create summary reports
What do I need to run IFTDSS
  • Landscape files and shapefiles can be downloaded directly within IFTDSS
  • Shapefiles can be created within IFTDSS Map Studio, or uploaded to IFTDSS from your local machine
